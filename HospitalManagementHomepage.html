Hospital Management System

Hospital Management System is a software application designed to manage the various aspects of a hospital's operations. It helps streamline administrative tasks, improve patient care, and enhance overall efficiency within the healthcare facility. The system typically includes modules for patient registration, appointment scheduling, medical records management, billing and invoicing, pharmacy management, laboratory management, and more. By automating these processes, hospital management systems aim to reduce paperwork, minimize errors, and provide healthcare professionals with easy access to critical information for better decision-making.
